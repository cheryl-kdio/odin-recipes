Nem rolls recipe

Description

Nem is a Vietnamese dish traditionally consisting of either minced prok, prawns or chicken wrapped in rice paper and deep fried.

Ingredients

  • either chicken breast, shrimp or pork
  • rice paper wrappers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 egg yolk
  • dry mushrooms
  • vermicelli
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic pouder
  • parsley

Steps

Step 1

  1. In a blender, mix the chicken breast and the onion.
  2. Grate the carrots.
  3. Soak vermicellis and dry mushrooms in boiling water for about 10min. When ready remove them and cut vermicellis into 2-inch long pieces.
  4. Put an egg yolk in a large bowl with all the previous aliments
  5. Add salt, pepper,garlic pouder,parsley and stir well.

Step 2

  1. Soak 1 rice paper wrapper in a shallow bowl of warm water to soften, about 15 seconds. Place on a damp cloth laid out on a flat surface.
  2. Place 1 tablespoon filling mixture into the center of softened rice paper. Fold the bottom edge into the center, covering filling. Fold in opposing edges and roll up tightly.
  3. Repeat with remaining rice paper wrappers, soaking and filling each one individually.

Step 3

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Working in batches of 3 or 4, fry spring rolls in hot oil until crisp and golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain on paper towels.
