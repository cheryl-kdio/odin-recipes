Stir-fried beef recipe

Description

Whip up a speedy beef, beansprout and spinach stir-fry seasoned with sweet mirin, garlic, sesame seeds and soy sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin or clear honey
  • 2 tsp cornflour, mixed to a paste with 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300g beef frying steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chilli, finely chopped
  • 3 spring onions, sliced into small pieces
  • 300g pack beansprout
  • 100g bag baby spinach
  • handful sesame seeds, toasted

Steps

Step 1

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the soy, mirin or honey, and cornflour paste.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan.
  3. Season the beef and sear in batches, then remove to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.
  4. Fry the garlic and chilli, then add the spring onions and beansprouts, and stir-fry for 2 mins.

Step 2

  1. Return the beef to the wok and add the spinach along with the sauce.
  2. Keep stir-frying for another 2-3 mins until the spinach has wilted.
  3. Serve sprinkled with the sesame seeds and some rice, if you like.
